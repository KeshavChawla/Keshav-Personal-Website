A Step by Step Guide to Build your own Smart Mirror

In July of 2017, I stumbled across photos and videos online of people making mirrors that could present a dashboard of widgets ranging from daily weather to any traffic delays.

I set out to build my own in the process using tutorials online and based on the MagicMirror² platform run on Electron.

In the process, I was able to work with PubNub's API Suite, Integrating Google's Assistant Voice in the mirror, MagicMirror²'s software, and Canadian news RSS feeds.

Tech Stack: Python, PubNub API
