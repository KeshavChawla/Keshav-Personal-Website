HackTheFridge - DoTheDew - Hackathon Project Winner
Tech Stack: Xcode, Arduino, PHP, and Indico's Image Recognition API

As part of the HackThe6ix hackathon, I worked with a team to create a marketing tool for Pepsi.

Inspired by the "I am Canadian" - Molson Canada Fridge Commercial , we converted the concept over for Pepsi/Mountain Dew into an app which gives users a list of tasks (scavenger hunt items) that need to be completed.

Once the tasks have been completed, a fridge is unlocked to receive free Mountain Dew!
